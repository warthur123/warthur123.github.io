Schedule of Planet Terp

When registering for classes, UMD students need to access many different online resources to obtain information on available classes, professor ratings and class difficulty. To address this issue of scattered information, My INST377 team and I developed a website that combines course information, course GPA and professor reviews into a single easy to use application. Our website draws data from UMD.io and PlanetTerp APIs to create a tool that makes class scheduling more efficient for UMD students.
